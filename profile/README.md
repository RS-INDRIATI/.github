## 🌐 English Version

### About Us

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **RS Indriati Boyolali**. We lead digital transformation in regional healthcare by building secure, interoperable, and user-centered software solutions that improve patient care, support clinical staff, and enable data-driven decision making.

Our repositories host components, integration tools, and documentation for systems used in the hospital environment. We maintain a mix of open-source utilities and internally managed projects that together form an Integrated Healthcare Information System.

### 🎯 Our Mission

To design, develop, and operate world-class integrated healthcare information systems that:
- Enhance patient safety with accurate, auditable clinical data
- Optimize clinical and administrative workflows through automation
- Protect patient data and ensure regulatory compliance
- Enable interoperability between departments and national health exchanges
- Foster continuous innovation and collaboration with the healthcare community

### 💻 Technology Stack

We primarily work with:
- Backend: Java (Spring), PHP
- Standards: HL7, FHIR, ICD-10, SNOMED CT
- Databases: PostgreSQL, MySQL, (Oracle where required)
- Integration: RESTful APIs, SOAP, Message Queues (RabbitMQ/ActiveMQ)
- DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes (where applicable), CI/CD, automated testing
- Security: TLS, encryption at rest, RBAC, audit logging

### 🏗️ Core Projects

Key systems and components maintained by our teams:
- Electronic Medical Records (EMR/EHR)
- Hospital Management Information System (HMIS/SIMRS)
- Laboratory Information System (LIS)
- Radiology Information System (RIS)
- Pharmacy Management System
- Clinical Decision Support (CDS)
- Analytics & Business Intelligence
- Patient Portal & Mobile Health Apps
- Telemedicine Platform

## 🌐 Versi Bahasa Indonesia

### Tentang Kami

Selamat datang di organisasi GitHub resmi **RS Indriati Boyolali**. Kami memimpin transformasi digital layanan kesehatan di wilayah kami dengan menghadirkan solusi perangkat lunak yang aman, dapat berinteroperasi, dan berfokus pada pengguna untuk meningkatkan pelayanan pasien dan mendukung tenaga kesehatan.

Repositori kami berisi komponen sistem, alat integrasi, dan dokumentasi yang digunakan di lingkungan rumah sakit. Kami mengelola kombinasi utilitas open-source dan proyek internal yang menjadi bagian dari Sistem Informasi Kesehatan Terintegrasi.

### 🎯 Misi Kami

Merancang, mengembangkan, dan mengoperasikan sistem informasi kesehatan terintegrasi yang:
- Meningkatkan keselamatan pasien dengan data klinis yang akurat dan dapat diaudit
- Mengoptimalkan alur kerja klinis dan administrasi melalui otomasi
- Melindungi data pasien dan memastikan kepatuhan regulasi
- Memungkinkan interoperabilitas antar departemen dan pertukaran data nasional
- Mendukung inovasi dan kolaborasi berkelanjutan di komunitas kesehatan

### 💻 Teknologi yang Kami Gunakan

Kami bekerja dengan teknologi:
- Backend: Java (Spring), PHP
- Standar: HL7, FHIR, ICD-10, SNOMED CT
- Database: PostgreSQL, MySQL (dan Oracle jika diperlukan)
- Integrasi: RESTful API, SOAP, Message Queue (RabbitMQ/ActiveMQ)
- DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes (jika diperlukan), CI/CD, automated testing
- Keamanan: TLS, enkripsi data, RBAC, audit logging

### 🏗️ Proyek Utama

Sistem dan komponen utama yang kami kelola:
- Rekam Medis Elektronik (RME/EMR)
- Sistem Informasi Manajemen Rumah Sakit (SIMRS/HMIS)
- Sistem Informasi Laboratorium (SIL)
- Sistem Informasi Radiologi (SIR)
- Sistem Manajemen Farmasi
- Sistem Pendukung Keputusan Klinis
- Analitik & Business Intelligence
- Portal Pasien & Aplikasi Mobile
- Platform Telemedicine
