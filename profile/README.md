## 🌐 English Version

### About Us

Welcome to the official GitHub organization of **RS Indriati Boyolali**. We are pioneering digital transformation in Indonesian healthcare through cutting-edge software engineering and integrated healthcare information systems.

As a leading healthcare institution, we are committed to developing robust, scalable, and secure digital solutions that enhance patient care, streamline clinical workflows, and support evidence-based medical practice.

### 🎯 Our Mission

To deliver world-class integrated healthcare information systems that:
- **Enhance Patient Safety** through accurate, real-time medical data management
- **Optimize Clinical Operations** with efficient workflow automation
- **Ensure Data Security** complying with healthcare cybersecurity standards
- **Enable Interoperability** across all hospital departments and external health systems
- **Support Innovation** through continuous digital transformation

### 💻 Technology Stack

Our development team specializes in:

- **Backend Development**: Java Enterprise Edition, Spring Framework, PHP
- **Healthcare Standards**: HL7, FHIR, ICD-10, SNOMED CT
- **Database Systems**: PostgreSQL, MySQL, Oracle
- **Security & Compliance**: Healthcare data protection, audit trail systems
- **Integration**: RESTful APIs, SOAP Web Services, Message Queuing
- **DevOps**: CI/CD pipelines, containerization, automated testing

### 🏗️ Core Projects

Our repositories contain components of our **Integrated Healthcare Information System** including:

- Electronic Medical Records (EMR/EHR)
- Hospital Management Information System (HMIS/SIMRS)
- Laboratory Information System (LIS)
- Radiology Information System (RIS)
- Pharmacy Management System
- Clinical Decision Support Systems
- Healthcare Analytics & Business Intelligence
- Patient Portal & Mobile Health Applications
- Telemedicine Platform

## 🌐 Versi Bahasa Indonesia

### Tentang Kami

Selamat datang di organisasi GitHub resmi **RS Indriati Boyolali**. Kami menjadi pelopor transformasi digital dalam layanan kesehatan Indonesia melalui rekayasa perangkat lunak terdepan dan sistem informasi kesehatan terintegrasi.

Sebagai institusi kesehatan terkemuka, kami berkomitmen mengembangkan solusi digital yang tangguh, terukur, dan aman untuk meningkatkan pelayanan pasien, merampingkan alur kerja klinis, dan mendukung praktik medis berbasis bukti.

### 🎯 Misi Kami

Menghadirkan sistem informasi kesehatan terintegrasi berkelas dunia yang:
- **Meningkatkan Keselamatan Pasien** melalui pengelolaan data medis yang akurat dan real-time
- **Mengoptimalkan Operasional Klinis** dengan otomasi alur kerja yang efisien
- **Menjamin Keamanan Data** sesuai standar keamanan siber kesehatan
- **Memungkinkan Interoperabilitas** di seluruh departemen rumah sakit dan sistem kesehatan eksternal
- **Mendukung Inovasi** melalui transformasi digital berkelanjutan

### 💻 Teknologi yang Kami Gunakan

Tim pengembangan kami berspesialisasi dalam:

- **Pengembangan Backend**: Java Enterprise Edition, Spring Framework, PHP
- **Standar Kesehatan**: HL7, FHIR, ICD-10, SNOMED CT
- **Sistem Database**: PostgreSQL, MySQL, Oracle
- **Keamanan & Kepatuhan**: Perlindungan data kesehatan, sistem audit trail
- **Integrasi**: RESTful APIs, SOAP Web Services, Message Queuing
- **DevOps**: CI/CD pipelines, containerization, automated testing

### 🏗️ Proyek Utama

Repositori kami berisi komponen dari **Sistem Informasi Kesehatan Terintegrasi** meliputi:

- Rekam Medis Elektronik (RME/EMR)
- Sistem Informasi Manajemen Rumah Sakit (SIMRS/HMIS)
- Sistem Informasi Laboratorium (SIL)
- Sistem Informasi Radiologi (SIR)
- Sistem Manajemen Farmasi
- Sistem Pendukung Keputusan Klinis
- Analitik Kesehatan & Business Intelligence
- Portal Pasien & Aplikasi Mobile Health
- Platform Telemedicine
